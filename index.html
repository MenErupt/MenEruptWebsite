Our Mission

Arise and Establish

Men Erupt is a movement of men committed to rising up in prayer and establishing God's kingdom on earth. We believe that when men unite in prayer, breakthrough happens.

Drawing from Micah 7:8 - "Do not gloat over me, my enemy! Though I have fallen, I will rise" - and 1 Peter 5:10 - "And the God of all grace... will himself restore you and make you strong, firm and steadfast."

We are called to be men of prayer, men of purpose, and men who establish God's will in our families, communities, and nations.
